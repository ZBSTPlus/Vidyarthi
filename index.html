About

Founder

Our founder, Tirupal Pulivarthi, is a renowned career counselor and mentor with 18 years of experience in the industry. He holds an MBA, B.Ed., NET, PGDYV, MA and is a Certified Career Counsellor (IC3 & Bodhami). He is also a Counselling Partner with Bohami, a Certified NLP Practitioner, and a Life Coach. His experience includes 3 years of Corporate Experience and 15 years of teaching Experience.
